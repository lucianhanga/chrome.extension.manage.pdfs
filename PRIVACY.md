# Privacy Policy — PDF Manager

_Last updated: 2026-06-11_

PDF Manager is a Chrome extension that lets you assemble, preview, and export
PDF files. This policy explains what the extension does and does not do with
your data.

## Summary

**PDF Manager does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on remote
servers. All processing happens locally in your browser.**

## What data the extension handles

- **PDF files you open** are read and processed entirely within your browser
  using in-memory operations and the WebAssembly PDF engine bundled with the
  extension. They are never uploaded anywhere.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not send your files or any portion of their contents over the network.
- It does not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or telemetry.
- It does not collect personally identifiable information.
- It does not sell or share data with third parties, because it collects none.

## Permissions

- **`contextMenus`** — used only to add a right-click menu entry for opening the
  extension. It does not read page content.

The extension requests **no host permissions** and has **no access to the
websites you visit**.

## Network access

PDF Manager makes no network requests. It functions fully offline.

## Changes to this policy

If the extension's data practices ever change, this document will be updated and
the "Last updated" date revised.
